Transdown

A lightweight syntax for transcripts of qualitative data.

Try it »

Simple

Transdown uses simple syntactical conventions, making it easy for humans to read and write

Powerful

Transdown comes with built-in support for common transcript elements including timestamps, speaker names, and associated media like images, animations, and sounds.

Extensible

Transdown can grow and respond to community needs over time to support a larger set of linguistic features
